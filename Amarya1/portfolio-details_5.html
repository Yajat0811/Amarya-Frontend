Services Details

Biopesticide Consulting

  • Category: Consulting(Technical)

Here is the detailed description of our Service

  • Biopesticide Evaluation: Our team evaluates a wide range of biopesticide products available in the market which help us in assessing their efficiency, safety, mode of action, compatibility with organic farming practices, and suitability for specific pests and crops.
  • Product Recommendations: Based on our evaluation done by our team we prepare a pest management plan and provide recommendations on the most suitable biopesticide products for your organic farm.
  • Application Techniques: We offer guidance on the proper application techniques for biopesticides to maximize their effectiveness and provides recommendations on application rates, timing, and methods to ensure accurate and efficient application, minimizing wastage and maximizing pest control results
  • Regulatory Compliance: Our team ensures that the biopesticides recommended for your farm comply with organic certification standards and regulatory guidelines, giving you peace of mind in terms of compliance and certification.
  • Training and Education: Our team provides education to farmers on the proper handling, storage, and application of biopesticides to ensure their safe and effective use.
