Services Details

Integrated Pest Management

  • Category: Technical

Here is the detailed description of our Service

  • Site Assessment We conduct a thorough assessment of your farm site to identify potential pest issues and develop an effective pest management plan.
  • Pest Monitoring: We utilize various monitoring methods to track pest populations and activity. This allows us to detect and respond to pest issues before they escalate and cause significant damage.
  • Natural Pest Control: We utilize natural pest control methods, such as biological control, cultural control, and physical control, to minimize the use of chemicals and ensure sustainable pest management practices.
  • Training and Education: : We provide training and education to farmers on pest management practices and techniques.
  • Continuous Monitoring and Evaluation: We also continuously monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of the pest management plan, making necessary adjustments and clarifications to ensure optimal pest control.
